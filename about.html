Our Story

From humble beginnings to a thriving yoga community, our journey has been one of growth and connection.

2015: Foundation

Our studio opened its doors with just one classroom and three instructors, offering basic yoga classes to the local community.

2017: First Expansion

We doubled our space and added specialized classes including prenatal yoga and yoga for athletes.

2019: Teacher Training

Launched our certified yoga teacher training program, graduating our first class of 12 instructors.

2021: Online Platform

Expanded our reach globally by introducing virtual classes and on-demand yoga sessions.

2023: Community Center

Opened our wellness center with holistic services including meditation spaces and nutritional counseling.
